REU Work

During the summer of 2023, I participated in a research experience for undergraduates (REU) program at the University of Wyoming. I assisted a project that involved developing a novel assistance system for robotic arm control, specificall I assisted with the refinement of the machine learning model used.
This was a great experience for me as it allowed me to learn about machine learning and how it can be applied to real-world problems. I also got to work alongside graduate students and learn about the research process. The specific packages I got experience with were tensorflow and pytorch, and I learned about basic neural network concepts.
