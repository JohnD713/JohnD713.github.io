GIS Project

Led a team to develop a custom hardware device and accompanying app for collecting location and temperature data that was then used by the college's GIS program.


This taught me a lot about agile project management and working within a team, as well as how to use the arduino IDE. I also got to learn about the GIS program at my college and how they use data to create maps and conduct research.
